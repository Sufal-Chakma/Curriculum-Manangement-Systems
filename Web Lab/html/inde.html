About Us

The Department of Fisheries and Marine Resources Technology of the Rangamati Science and Technology University is one of the leading Fisheries and Marine Resources Technology Departments in Bangladesh. The department was established in 2020 with the vision of producing high-quality fisheries graduates. Presently the Department has 25 students. The Department offers 4-year Bachelor degree in Fisheries.Read More
