About Us

The Department of Management commenced its journey in 2014. Currently Department offers four years undergraduate BBA program, one year MBA program under the current syllabus. There are 9 teachers in the Department, of which 8 are Assistant Professors and 1 lecturers. Maximum of the teachers are trained and most of them have a good number of research publications. Things are changing very fast and a degree in Management will help you acquire required skills to meet the challenges in the years to come. We are offering a variety of degrees- BBA, MBA.
