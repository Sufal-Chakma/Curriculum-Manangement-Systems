The vision of the Department of Forestry and Environmental Sciences is produce quality graduates with sound knowledge and skills to achieve sustainable development in the areas of forestry and environmental sciences. The mission statement of the institute is provide excellent teaching-learning integrated with relevant research and practical experiences that will prepare students to be innovative and knowledgeable in the fields of forestry and environmental sciences by producing leaders and agents to address the local and global issues in order to ensure conservation of forest and environment as well as to mitigate the climate change hazards.Read More
