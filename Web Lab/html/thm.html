About Us

The Department of Tourism and Hospitality Management (THM) offers a 4-year graduation program followed by a one-year Master's degree. The graduates have a range of opportunities to explore the national and international arena of tourism and hospitality - one of the fastest-growing industries of the globe. The remarkable wings of the industry include travel agencies and tour operations, hotels and resorts, airlines, amusement parks, food services, tourism education, and research institutes, event management organizations, cruise ships, tour guides, national and international tourism organizations etc. The graduates are encouraged to be tourism developers providing a range of knowledge and skills during their study.
